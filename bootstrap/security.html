Energy Security

Energy Visualization in Europe

See the visualization

Energy security has many aspects: long-term energy security mainly deals with timely investments to supply energy in line with economic developments and environmental needs. On the other hand, short-term energy security focuses on the ability of the energy system to react promptly to sudden changes in the supply-demand balance.

In the wake of the war in Ukraine, energy security has become an imminent issue. We will show the data of energy import and export of each country by sankey chart. If the energy import of a country is much larger than the export, we think there is instability in energy security of this country.
