Renewable energy

Renewable energy is energy that has been derived from earth's natural resources that are not finite or exhaustible, such as wind and sunlight. Renewable energy is an alternative to the traditional energy that relies on fossil fuels, and it tends to be much less harmful to the environment.

Renewable energy is considered as a crucial resource for sustainable development. We visualize top 10 EU countries with the highest share of clean energy use year by year.

It can be seen that overall Iceland (code: IC) and Norway (code: NO) replace approximately 60% of their energy sources with renewable ones.Scandinavian countries are ahead of Europe in using clean energy.
